Sarma - Croatian Cabbage Rolls

We see fireworks in the sky, and we Croatians see Sarma (cabbage rolls) on our plates!

There is no better time to come to a Croatian household than the day after New Year, when everyone is eating an absolute favourite of all generations that will always gather all around the table, especially during winter.

They are sauerkraut cabbage rolls, which will bring all the flavors and combine sweet, spicy and savory. There is not a single person that will not try to give you advice on how to make it. (as every author of the recipe will always mention as well).

This is Balkans way to give you a warm welcome and a family hug, type of meal!
